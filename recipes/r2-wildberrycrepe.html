Wildberry Crepe

Wild, sweet, delicious dessert for breakfast!

Absolutely decadent in both appearance and deliciousness! This sugary is both filling and healthy, using berries found in the cold and frozen wilds to enhance the sweetness! Discovered by link while assisting a confectioner survive her extreme foragging efforts for this delectible berry, which are sweetest wherever it's very cold.

On wildberries

"A fruit that grows in cold, snowy regions known for its tangy, sweet flavor. It doesn't offer any special effects, but it's a popular cooking ingredient."

Ingredients:

  • (1) Bird Egg
  • (1) Fresh Milk
  • (1) Tabantha Wheat
  • (1) Cane Sugar
  • (1) Wildberry

Steps

  1. Prepare cooking wok
  2. Light fire (underneath wok)
  3. Gather ingredient in arms
  4. Toss ingredients in wok
  5. Allow ingredients to bounce and cook unimpeded
  6. Food should cook quickly. A final bounce and a puff of smoke should indicate when it finished!
  7. A small hop of celebration helps really drive home the feeling of accomplishment and flavor!
