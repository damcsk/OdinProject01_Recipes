Gourmet Poultry Curry

Savory and Spicy Fowl!

Once served in Hyrule Castle, the poultry (a whole bird!) used in this dish is of immensely high quality. The savory meat pairs well with the aroma of spice in this common curry.

On Goron Spices:

"Made from several types of spices, this secret Goron seasoning has been handed down for generations. An initial wave of spiciness paves the way for sweetness."

Ingredients:

  • (1) Raw Whole Bird
  • (1) Hylian Rice
  • (1+) Goron Spice

Steps

  1. Prepare cooking wok
  2. Light fire (underneath wok)
  3. Gather ingredient in arms
  4. Toss ingredients in wok
  5. Allow ingredients to bounce and cook unimpeded
  6. Food should cook quickly. A final bounce and a puff of smoke should indicate when it finished!
  7. A small hop of celebration helps really drive home the feeling of accomplishment and flavor!
